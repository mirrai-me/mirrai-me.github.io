2. Collection and storage of personal data, as well as the nature and purpose of its use

a) When visiting the website

When you visit our website mirrai.me, the browser used on your device automatically sends information to our website's server. This information is temporarily stored in so-called log files.

The following are recorded, for example:

  • IP address of the requesting computer
  • Date and time of access
  • Name and URL of the file accessed
  • Website from which access is made (referrer URL)
  • Browser used and, if applicable, operating system of your computer

This data is used for system security and stability as well as to optimize the website. Processing is carried out in accordance with Art. 6 para. 1 lit. f GDPR.

Hosting is provided by GitHub Pages (GitHub Inc., 88 Colin P. Kelly Jr. Street, San Francisco, CA 94107, USA). More information: GitHub Privacy Statement .

b) When using the contact form (Formspree)

When you use our contact or waiting list form, your details (e.g., name, email address) are forwarded to us via the Formspree service (Formspree Inc., Delaware, USA). Data processing is carried out in accordance with Art. 6 (1) (b) or (a) GDPR. Further information can be found at: https://formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy/ .
